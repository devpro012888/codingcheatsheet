<p> A paragraph consists of one or more sentences that form a self-contained unit of disclosure. The start of a paragraph is indicated by a new line. To create a paragraph, surround the words that make up the paragraph with an opening <p> tag and closing </p> tag.
<b> This is how we make words appear bold By enclosing words in the tags <b> and </b> we can make characters appear bold.
<i> This is how we make a word appear italic By enclosing words in the tags <i> and <i/> we can make characters appear italic.
<sup> E=MC2 The <sup> element is used to contain characters that should be superscript such as the suffixes of dates or mathematical concepts like raising a number to a power such as 22.
<sub> CO2 The <sub> element is used to contain characters that should be subscript. It is commonly used with foot notes or chemical formulas such as H2O.
